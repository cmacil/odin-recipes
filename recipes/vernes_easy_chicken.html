Verne's Easy Chicken

Description

A simple recipe for a quick meal! Stop off at a roadside supermarket, have the rear seat passengers mix up the marinade (they'd better watch the leather back there!), and have the navigator guard the soon-to-be-divine chicken. They should be able to get all of that done while you preheat the oven/engine on the way to the bbq ground. That's the hard part anyway!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 Tbsp honey
  • 1 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients and marinate chicken for 1 hour.
  2. Place on grille over direct heat or under your ovens broiler.
  3. Turn occasionally until cooked through.
